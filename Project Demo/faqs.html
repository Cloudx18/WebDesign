Frequently Asked Questions


What is Silver City Theatre?

Silver City Theatre allows everyone to enjoy vintage movies in the movie theatre. We strive to bring the best and memorable movies to relieve your stress and make the experience as unique as possible!


How do Silver City Theatre screening work?

Silver City Theatre screening takes place, as long as there is reservations. Each screening has its own web page where you'll find an update that show you the number of tickets left for that screening.

Our theatre fits a total of 30 people, when the tickets are all sold out, no extra screening will be available. Hence, we remind you to reserve the tickets as soon as possible to avoid any disappointment.

What should I do to maximise my movie experience?

I'm glad you asked! Here are some basic manners that can make the experience more pleasant for you and other movie-goers:

  • Don't be late for your movie.
  • Buy your refreshment before your movie.
  • Talking is impolite and so is whispering.
  • Put your phone on silent vibrant (silent mode would be the best!).
